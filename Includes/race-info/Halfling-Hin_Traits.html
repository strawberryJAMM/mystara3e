Hin characters have racial traits identical to those of the core halfling race as specified in the Players Handbook [PHB], except as follows:

  • Regions: (The Known World) Although the largest concentrations are, of course, found in the Hin homeland of The Five Shires, pockets of Hin can be found throughout this region of  Mystara. The most common countries that an adventuring Hin might be from include: Darokin; Five Shires, Karameikos; Minrothad (esp. Open Isle); Rockhome and Thyatis. One country a Hin is not likely to be from is Glantri, where Halflings have sometimes been treated as little more than lab specimens by wizards hoping to learn the secrets behind their race's innate resistance to spells and energy damage.
  • Woodland Blending: +2 racial bonus to Hide checks when in an outdoor area of woods or underbrush.
  • Automatic Languages: Local Regional Language and Local Common (if different from Local Regional Language).
  • Bonus Languages: Dwarven, Elven, Gnome, Lalor (Halfling), Local Regional and Local Goblinoid languages. (Exact list of available bonus local languages varies with region.)
