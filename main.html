Welcome Traveler...

ou have stumbled across the semiofficial home of Mystara™ for the Third Edition of Dungeons & Dragons (which we fondly refer to as Mystara-3E). On this site we provide game rules and related information that a DM can use to start and run a Third Edition Dungeons & Dragons campaign in any of the three campaign settings that make up the realms of Mystara: The Known World, The Savage Coast, and The Hollow World.

The conversion on this web site has been upgraded for use with the Dungeons & Dragons™ Core Books, Edition 3.5, published by Wizards of the Coast, Inc., although it can also be used with the Dungeons & Dragons™ Third Edition Core Books if desired

The Immortals Above [updated: Jun 19, 2003]

Mystara, unlike most fantasy settings, has no Gods to interfere in the affairs of mortals on a regular basis. Mystara is blessed, instead, with a multitude of Immortals: incredibly powerful beings who are worshipped by mortals as deities, but who were once mortal themselves and who only occasionally interfere in the affairs of mortals

This section contains 3E conversions for each of the many Immortals found throughout Mystara which, for most 3E purposes, fill the same niche that the Gods and Goddesses do in other 3E fantasy settings, including details on the domains that they each provide to their clerics. [More...]

Mystaran Multiverse [updated: Feb 01, 2004]

Mystara is situated in a completely different dimension from that of the default Dungeons & Dragons campaign setting.  Where the default campaign setting is located in the Material Plane of a dimension that conforms to the "Great Wheel Cosmology", Mystara is located in the Prime Plane, a Material Plane in a dimension known as the Multiverse.

This section contains details of the Mystaran Multiverse cosmology that conforms and integrates with the conventions presented in Dungeons & Dragons: Manual of the Planes [MotP] and in the section of the The d20 System Reference Document [d20-SRD] on the planes. [More...]
